About GST Reports
GSTR-1 (Outward Supplies)

This report includes details of all outward supplies made during the specified period. Use this for filing monthly/quarterly GSTR-1 returns.

  • B2B invoices (Business to Business)
  • B2C invoices (Business to Consumer)
  • HSN summary
GSTR-3B (Summary Return)

This is a summary return of outward and inward supplies, ITC claimed, and tax payment details. Use this for filing monthly GSTR-3B returns.

  • Summary of outward supplies and tax liability
  • Inter-state supplies to unregistered persons
  • Tax payment summary
B2B Invoices

Invoices issued to GST registered businesses. Receiver's GSTIN is captured and tax is reported at invoice level.

B2C Invoices

Invoices issued to non-registered customers. Reported in aggregate for intra-state and at invoice level for inter-state.

HSN Summary

Summary of sales based on HSN (Harmonized System of Nomenclature) codes, aggregated by tax rates.
