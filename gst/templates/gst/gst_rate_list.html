About GST Rates

GST (Goods and Services Tax) in India is categorized into the following main slabs:

  • 0% (Nil Rate): Essential goods like fresh vegetables, milk, etc.
  • 5%: Items of mass consumption like sugar, tea, spices, etc.
  • 12%: Processed food, computers, mobiles, etc.
  • 18%: Most manufactured items and services.
  • 28%: Luxury items like cars, aerated drinks, etc.
  • Special Rates: Gold (3%), precious stones (0.25%), etc.

Notes:

  • For intra-state transactions, GST is divided equally between CGST (Central GST) and SGST (State GST).
  • For inter-state transactions, only IGST (Integrated GST) is applicable, which equals CGST + SGST.
  • HSN (Harmonized System of Nomenclature) codes are used to classify goods under GST.
