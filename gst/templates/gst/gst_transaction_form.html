GST Transaction Information
Transaction Types:
  • Sales: For outward supplies of goods or services
  • Purchase: For inward supplies of goods or services
  • Credit Note: For return of goods or price adjustments (reduction in value)
  • Debit Note: For additional charges or price adjustments (increase in value)
Intra-State vs Inter-State:
  • Intra-State: When buyer and seller are in the same state (CGST + SGST applies)
  • Inter-State: When buyer and seller are in different states (IGST applies)
B2B vs B2C:
  • B2B (Business to Business): For registered dealers with GSTIN (GST Input Tax Credit available)
  • B2C (Business to Consumer): For unregistered customers (No GST Input Tax Credit)
