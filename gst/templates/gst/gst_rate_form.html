GST Rate Information
CGST and SGST rates are typically equal, and together they equal the IGST rate. For example, if CGST is 9% and SGST is 9%, the IGST rate should be 18%.

HSN Code: Harmonized System of Nomenclature (HSN) is a 6-digit uniform code that classifies goods and services for taxation. It helps in standardizing GST applications across India.
