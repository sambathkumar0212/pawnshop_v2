Help
Tiered Interest Rate System

This scheme uses a three-period loan structure with different interest rates based on when the customer repays the loan:

  • Early Period: If a customer repays within this initial period, they benefit from a reduced interest rate.
  • Standard Period: If repayment occurs during this middle period, the regular interest rate applies.
  • Late Period: If the customer delays beyond the standard period, a higher interest rate is applied.
Interest Rate Details
  • Early Rate: Lower rate to incentivize quick repayment
  • Standard Rate: Normal interest rate for the scheme
  • Late Rate: Higher rate that applies to delayed repayments
Setting Periods
  • Total Loan Duration: Must exceed the sum of early and standard periods
  • Minimum Duration: The minimum time a loan must remain active
  • Period Balance: Adjust periods to encourage timely repayment
Tip: Set the early period interest rate lower to incentivize quick repayment, and the late period rate higher to discourage defaults.
