Scheme Settings Guide
Creating Gold Loan Schemes

Gold loan schemes use monthly interest rates per ₹100 of loan amount. The system will automatically calculate the annual interest rate.

Field Explanations
  • Gold Interest Rate: Monthly interest in rupees per ₹100
  • Expiry Period: Loan term in months
  • Processing Fee: Percentage of loan amount charged upfront
  • Amount Range: Min and max loan amount allowed
  • Special Conditions: Any additional terms or rules
Example

A gold loan scheme with interest rate of 1.00 means ₹1 per ₹100 per month, which equals 12% annual interest.
